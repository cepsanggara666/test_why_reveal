Overview

Retrowave deer is a collection of 7,777 NFT’s with iconic items from the 80’s. Owning a retrowave deer NFT provides you with access to the NFT game from the 80’s and exclusive merch. Every deer is unique with varying traits created by a generative algorithm and registered on the Ethereum blockchain.

Our team is aiming to launch a fully-functional NFT game. 5% of collection sales will be used to increase the floor price of the whole collection (aka buyback).
